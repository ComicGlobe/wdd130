Overview

Purpose

The intended ojective of our website is to attract new customers and make it easier for existing customers to share our business in an easy and informative manner.

Audience

The target audience of our website are high adventure adults.

  • What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? The adience should expect to find details of what our company is all about, as well as how to contact us, and where we are located.
  • What do they want? The customers want to be able to book a time to go on a white water rafting adventure.
  • How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? Since we will be pushing the majority of our advertisments through facebook, it will be a mix. However, the majority of people will be accessing our website on a desktop or laptop computer.
